  • OUR VALUES

    • Introduction


    At Hot Beans, we believe in building more than just websites, we create innovative digital experiences that shape the future. As a growing web development company, we’re on the lookout for passionate, creative, and skilled individuals to join our dynamic team. Whether you’re an experienced developer, designer, or strategist, we offer an exciting environment where you can thrive, grow, and make a real impact. At Hot Beans, you’ll work on cutting-edge projects, collaborate with talented professionals, and have the opportunity to continuously learn and evolve in the fast-paced world of web development. Join us and be a part of something great!

  • WE CAN OFFER YOU THE BEST PERKS

    • Why Choose Us?




    Experience


    Hot beans has some of the most experienced web developers and UI/UX designs there are. You will be surrounded by knowledgable and patient people the second you join our team, so don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it!


    Communication


    Having good people skills is necessary for a web developer job. This is because you will spend 50% of your time working with client's trying to better understand the brief. We will guide you to become a master at extracting the necessary information you need to start building your client's dreams!


    Flexibility


    Tired of having to drive everyday at work? Well, you've found the right job for you. At Hot Beans most of our developers either solely work from home or are hybrid workers. We also hold nearly all of our meetings over the internet so there's no need to travel to our office everyday!

  • LEARN HOW TO WORK IN INDUSTRY

    • What You'll Be Doing



    Learning to extract key information from client


    It's important to be good at this as most clients don't understand what we as web developers need to create a professional and informative site for their users




    Designing beautiful and responsive web applications and sites


    Making a site / web app aesthetically pleasing to the eye is proven to keep users engaged, and responsiveness is a must as over 50% of traffic is from mobile users




    Developing / designing an app or website to meet client needs using HTML, CSS and JavaScript


    If you join our team at Hot Beans you'll be doing mostly front-end work using the above web languages and or designing these apps and sites. You will learn on the job from our experienced members so only a basic level of knowledge is needed!
