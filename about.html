  • OUR HISTORY

    • About Hot Beans


    Founded in 2008, Hot Beans began as a small team of passionate web developers with a shared vision of creating impactful digital experiences. Over the years, we have grown into a trusted leader in the web development industry, building innovative solutions for businesses of all sizes. Through our dedication to quality, creativity, and client satisfaction, we have successfully completed hundreds of projects, earning a reputation for excellence. Our journey is one of continuous learning and adaptation, driven by the ever-evolving world of technology and the desire to help our clients succeed online.




  • WHAT WE WANT TO ACHIEVE

    • Our Mission


    Our mission is to empower businesses of all sizes by delivering innovative, user-centric web solutions that drive growth and digital transformation. In the future, we aim to lead the industry by continuously pushing the boundaries of technology, design, and development, ensuring our clients stay ahead in a fast-changing digital world. We are committed to fostering long-term relationships, providing exceptional value, and helping organizations unlock their full potential through cutting-edge websites and digital experiences

  • CAN WE ANSWER YOUR QUESTIONS?

    • Frequently Asked Questions


    If I join as a junior level developer what will my starting salary be?

    Anywhere between £25,000 - £32,000

    How can I join the Hot Beans team?

    1. Make sure you are qualified for the role. Go to Job Specifications > required qualifications to see if you are eligible

    2. Apply using the form located on the home page, must have a CV to upload so we can check your credentials

    3. Hot Beans recruitment team will overlook your CV and see if you are the right fit for us!

    Will I receive training even though I have the required qualifications?

    No, you should have a fairly in-depth knowledge into web developement and all that surrounds it, hence why we require a minimum qualification level. However, bi-yearly training is mandatory for ALL employees regardless of your prior knowledge so that developers can adjust to different frameworks/IDE that we have started using.

    Do you have any company perks / why should I choose Hot Beans?

    Yes! we offer 2 *FREE* hot drinks a day along with compulsory regular breaks from screens. We also have a small company car park based on site where you can park with no fee if you are an employee.
